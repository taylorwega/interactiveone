My family came to the Pacific Northwest from Norway in the late 1920s. They passed down tradtions of quiltmaking, needlework(Hardanger), and painting. As well as making delicious food. Beste, or Grandma, makes quilts at church with the quilting group. They are made out of love. With donated materials, they make a few hundred quilts every year.
